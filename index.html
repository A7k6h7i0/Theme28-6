Our Story

Redefining Luxury Real Estate

Founded in 2008, Prestige Estates was born from a vision to create an agency that truly understood the needs of discerning clientele. We set out not just to sell properties, but to curate life experiences — matching exceptional homes with extraordinary people.

Over 15 years, we've grown from a boutique Beverly Hills firm into a globally recognized luxury real estate brand operating across 48 markets. Yet our core philosophy remains unchanged: every client deserves personalized, premium service tailored to their unique vision.

Today, our award-winning team of over 200 specialists combines deep market expertise with an unwavering commitment to excellence, having facilitated over $2.4 billion in transactions.

Alexandra Prestige

Founder & CEO
